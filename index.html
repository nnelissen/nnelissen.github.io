About

Hi, I'm Natalie (she/her) and I'm a data geek by day and arts and crafts enthusiast by night. I have a background in neuroscience and data science, but I'm interested in all aspects of evidence-based working. I love crocheting, writing and teaching, and dabbling into various random hobbies. I'm in a full time job as a data scientist in the public sector, but I try to set aside some time for freelance not-for-proft projects. Spotting, exploring or solving a problem, improving the efficiency or effectiveness of a process or product, data visualisation and story telling? If it involves data or other evidence (it usually should), I'm happy to help.

You can find my CV here, connect on LinkedIn or check my scientific publications on Google Scholar.
